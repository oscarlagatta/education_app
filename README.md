# Features Overview

This document outlines the main features of our education app, designed with Test-Driven Development (TDD) and Clean Architecture to ensure reliability, scalability, and maintainability.

## Core Principles

- **TDD**: Development follows the Test-Driven Development methodology, ensuring every feature is tested before implementation.
- **Clean Architecture**: The app's structure is organized into separate layers, with clear boundaries and responsibilities, enhancing the separation of concerns and making the system more modular and adaptable to change.

## Main Features

### Authentication & Profile

- **Social Login**: Users can quickly sign in using their social media accounts, enhancing convenience and user experience.
- **Profile Center**: Users can create, delete, and update their profiles, including images, to maintain a personalized presence within the app.

### Course Interaction

- **View Course**: Users have access to a comprehensive list of courses, with detailed information to help them make informed decisions.
- **Join Group Chat**: Engage with other users by joining course-related group chats, fostering a collaborative learning environment.
- **Auto Create Group Chat**: When a course is initiated, a group chat is automatically created for participants, facilitating immediate communication.
- **Leave Group Chat**: Users can opt out of group chats at any time, giving them control over their messaging experience.

### Video Features

- **Upload Video from Backend**: Administrators can upload educational videos from the backend, enriching the course content available to users.
- **Play Video from Local Storage**: Users can download and play videos from their device's local storage, allowing for offline learning.

### Notifications

- **Receive Notification**: Users are notified of important events, messages, and updates, ensuring they stay informed.
- **Clear Notification**: Users can clear notifications, helping them manage their notification center effectively.

### Onboarding & Administration

- **Onboarding Screen**: First-time users are greeted with an onboarding screen, guiding them through the app’s features and functionalities.
- **Firebase Integration**: The app utilizes Firebase for backend services, including database, authentication, and analytics.
- **Admin Panel**: An admin panel is available for managing the app’s content, users, and settings, providing control and oversight.
- **Upload Exam**: Instructors can upload exams, allowing for comprehensive assessment of student learning.

## Conclusion

Our education app, built on the principles of TDD and Clean Architecture, offers a robust, user-friendly platform for online learning. With features ranging from social login to video playback and exam uploads, it's designed to enhance the educational experience for both students and educators.
